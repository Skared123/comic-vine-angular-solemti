SPIDER-MAN

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker's life changed forever. High school student by day, wall-crawler by night, he balances the weight of great power with great responsibility.

Origins & History

Peter Parker was an orphaned boy raised by his Aunt May and Uncle Ben in Queens, New York. A brilliant student with a natural affinity for science, Peter's life was forever altered during a school field trip to a research laboratory. While observing an experiment, a spider that had been accidentally irradiated bit him on the hand.
